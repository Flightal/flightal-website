## Section 2 — The problem

**Headline:** You're flying blind to most of what happens in your cockpit.

**Body:**
Your FDM captures thousands of aircraft parameters. It flags exceedances. It can't tell you why they happened.

Was the altitude bust a clearance error or a misheard ATC instruction? Was the unstable approach a tired crew or a runway change? Your FDM doesn't know. Neither does LOSA, it covered 5% of your flights. Neither do ASAP reports, they capture what pilots noticed and chose to report.

**Emphasis line:** The rest is invisible.
